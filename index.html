Efficiency Audit

Can LinkedIn drive low-cost signups for Engineers?

Targeting Software Engineers, SREs, and DevOps on LinkedIn is paradoxically expensive yet efficient. While raw CPC (Cost-Per-Click) is often 400% higher than Meta, the cost per activated user for PLG products is frequently lower.

The Friction Gap: Engineering signups fail on LinkedIn when ads look like "marketing." High-efficiency campaigns for this crowd focus on utility over aspiration.

Effective Tactics

  • • Single Image: Benchmarks & UI/UX
  • • Document Ads: Architecture diagrams
  • • Conversation: "Talk to a Founder" CTA

Ineffective Tactics

  • • Stock photos of people shaking hands
  • • Vague value props ("Save time!")
  • • Form fields asking for phone numbers
